ABOUT US

Computer Science is a rising field that is very important to our current society.

It's important that young women who are interested in computer science are given as many resources

as the young men so that they can succeed and the future has more women in computer science.

Colleges for GWC was created to be one of these many resources. We provide young women

with information on the Top Colleges for Computer Science, and which out of those

are the most affordable, if money is a concern. From there, we have them

submit their GPA and SAT scores to help narrow their options and

see where they can continue their education. Let's help expand

the Computer Science field to one that is representative of

both men and women.
